Global Economy

Introduction

This visualization focuses on the Global Economy. The main points of it are the GDP and GDP Per Capita of each country. And shows different perspectives of these main points by the following diagrams and maps.

World GDP in 2019

This map shows the GDP in each country in 2019. It clearly shows the spatial distribution in the world.

The GDP of most countries is less than 1,000,000 (1M in legend) million dollars.

Just three countries which are China, Japan, and the U.S.A have GDP higher than 5,000,000 (5M on map) million dollars.

Almost Africa country's GDP is less than 100,000 (100k in legend) million dollars.

World GDP Per Capita in 2019

The maximum distribution of GDP Per Capita in the wrold is between 15,000 to 1000 dollors. In South America and Asia, they have similar distribution .

The text of the country is the five countries with the highest GDP. Different from the U.S.A, Germany, and Japan, the World GDP Per Capita of China and India are lower than 15000 dollars.

Africa's GDP per capita is the lowest .

The Population v.s. Economy

This diagram shows the distribution when the GDP compares with GDP Per Capita and the relationship with the population.

The trend of this diagram is the GDP Per Capita is higher, the GDP also is higher. Also, it shows a smaller population has higher GDP Per Capita. Almost all countries meet these two trends.

However, there are two outliers which are just meet one of the trends. China is the second highest GDP country, but the GDP Per Capita is so low. Switzerland has the lower GDP in these 20 countries, but it has the highest GDP Per Capita.

Most countries' population are lower than 100,000,000 (100M in legend).
